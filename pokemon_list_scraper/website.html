This is a list of Pok\xc3\xa9mon in the order dictated by the National Pok\xc3\xa9dex, meaning that Pok\xc3\xa9mon from the Kanto region will appear first, followed by those from Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Kalos, Alola, and Galar. Each region\'s set of Pok\xc3\xa9mon starts with its own set of starter Pok\xc3\xa9mon and their evolutions, going in order of Grass, Fire, Water; the only exception is Unova, which begins with Victini, who is then followed by the starter Pok\xc3\xa9mon.\n

The first 151 entries in this Pok\xc3\xa9dex also serve as Kanto\'s regional Pok\xc3\xa9dex. In Generation II, this Pok\xc3\xa9dex order was known as the "Old Pok\xc3\xa9dex", with a new order that put related Pok\xc3\xa9mon near each other also included.\n
